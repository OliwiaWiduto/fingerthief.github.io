Environfriend

Details

Brief / Final Year Major Project

Role / Research, Information Architecture, UI/UX Design, Illustration

Software / Figma, Illustrator, Notion

Project Summary

For my major project I was given free reign over what I wanted to work on. I had just read Gerry McGovern's "World Wide Waste" and learned that the tech industry is responsible for 3.6% of global carbon emissions. I felt moved to take action.

Environfriend focuses heavily on behaviour change design, using the methodology to help the user track and measure their carbon emissions while also suggesting ways to reduce these.

To keep the user coming back, I chose a tamagotchi approach where the user has to take care of a pet. When they perform psoitive actions (reading about carbon emissions, staying on track with reducing their emissions) they earn Environcoins which they can exchgange for food or clean air for their pet.
